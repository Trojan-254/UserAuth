Our Story

Founded in 2023, ZetuCart emerged from a vision to create a seamless online shopping experience for Kenyans. We recognized the challenges faced by both consumers and sellers in the digital marketplace and set out to build a platform that would bridge these gaps.

Starting with just a handful of trusted sellers, we've grown to become one of Kenya's most trusted e-commerce platforms, serving thousands of customers daily and partnering with hundreds of verified vendors across the country.
